About Out Instructor

Kenyatta D. Gentry is a 2nd generation Lotus practitioner.
He currently holds a 3rd degree black belt with over 20 years' experience.
In 1988 He began LOTUS training with his father Jack J. Gentry, Charlotte North Carolina Professor of Lotus Self-defense and
1st degree black belt in Hapkido. Ajarn Ken participated in many martial arts tournaments throughout his career. He was awarded United States Junior Karate Circuit "competitor of the year" Southeastern division 1996-1998.
He has taught private self-defense classes to security staff at local night clubs, self-defense seminars at the Battered Woman's Shelter in Charlotte, and facilitated martial arts summer camps at local Presbyterian Churches in Charlotte. Ajarn Ken co-founded the LOTUS Martial Arts Academy located in Charlotte North Carolina and recently Founded Dragonfly Martial Arts & Fitness located in Gastonia, NC.
